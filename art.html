Arina The Artist

When I am not walking dogs or looking after animals I go by the name “Arina the Artist”. I am a self taught artist trained photographer who specialises in pet portraits, working in a range of mediums from colour pencil to oil paint!

I aim to capture a life like drawing of your beloved pet and hope to capture their unique personality in every portrait. These can be ordered from size A5 to size A3. I also offer pet photoshoot sessions and equine photography alongside my portraits. These are a great alternative to capture the unique bond between you and your pet!

All you need for a portrait is a selection of high quality images which can be sent to me via email and details of sizing and preference of medium.
